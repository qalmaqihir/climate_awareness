# GB Flood Situation — 2026 Snapshot

**Compiled:** 2026-07-22
**Purpose:** Confirm project relevance and urgency. Baseline for MVP content.

## Current status (2026 monsoon season)

- Pakistan Meteorological Department (PMD) issued its **second GLOF alert of June 2026** for GB and KP as sustained above-normal temperatures accelerated glacier + snowfield melt. Existing glacial lakes at risk.
- National Emergencies Operation Centre (NEOC / NDMA) advisory (Feb 2026) warned of **elevated GLOF risk March–September 2026**.
- Monsoon floods across Pakistan (as of July 22, 2026): **47 dead**, active flood alerts triggering evacuations.
- GB recorded **49 damaged houses + 2.52 km damaged roads** in early assessments.
- Karakoram Highway blocked at multiple locations by rain-triggered landslides; remote villages isolated.

## Affected areas documented

Flash floods reported in: **Darel, Khanbari, Buttogah, Khannar, Hodur, Niat, Thor** (Diamer district). Destroyed: standing crops, orchards, houses, connecting bridges, water channels, flour mills, rural roads.

Prior (2024): **Ghizer** valley cut off for 5+ days due to Gilgit-Shandur road blockage.

## Systemic failures reported by local media

Per Ibex Media Network investigation:
- Rs 10.3 billion allocated; only Rs 350 million spent on TADA (allowances).
- Hiring irregularities widespread.
- **200 Early Warning Systems planned** at Rs 160 million — none installed at scale.
- Target: 95% population flood-risk aware — not achieved.

Framing: report as data, not accusation. Neutral wording on site.

## Implications for MVP

1. **Timeliness is critical.** 2026 monsoon actively producing events. Site should launch before season peak (August).
2. **Neutral tone matters.** Existing coverage already carries systemic-failure narrative. Our value = aggregation, geography, timeline — not more critique.
3. **Multiple English sources exist.** Cross-verification possible without local language dependency for v1.
4. **Diamer + Ghizer districts** = highest recent incident density → seed content from these first.
5. **PMD + NDMA advisories are the alert baseline.** Cover these systematically; independent of volunteer curation.

## Open questions for later phases

- Historical GLOF archive: how far back can we go? ICIMOD HKH database has global inventory.
- AKAH 828-village hazard maps: can we get GeoJSON layer? Requires outreach (v3).
- Video content licensing: Pamir Times / Ibex — oEmbed handles FB/IG. YouTube separately.
